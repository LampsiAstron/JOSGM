Who We Are

WHERE LIVES ARE CHANGED




OUR VISION


Building God's Kingdom in Hearts, Homes, Communities and Beyond.



OUR MISSION


Grow and Equip people to maturity in Christ.
Connect people in meaningful relationships and ministries.
Mobilize people to live their God-given calling.
Plant sustainable churches in Towns, Cities and Barangays



OUR VALUES


  1. WORD BASED - BUILDING OUR LIVES ON GOD'S WORD
    • We value the Word of God as absolute Truth [ JOHN 17:17 ]
    • We value the Word of God as the Highest Authority [ 2 TIMOTHY 3:16 ]
    • We are committed to reading, studying and living by the Word [ COLOSSIANS 3:16 | 1 PETER 2:1-3 ]



    "I CANNOT LIVE WITHOUT THE WORD"



  2. SPIRIT LEAD - OBEYING THE PERSON OF THE HOLY SPIRIT
    • We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit [ ACTS 2:38 ]
    • We embrace the fellowship of the Holy Spirit [ JOHN 16:13 ]
    • We are led by the Holy Spirit in every aspect of our lives[ ROMANS 8:14 ]
    • We believe the gifts of the Holy Spirit are fully functional [ 1 CORINTHIANS 12 ]



    "I CANNOT LIVE WITHOUT THE FELLOWSHIP OF THE HOLY SPIRIT"



  3. FAMILY FOUNDED - COMMITTING OURSELVES TO BUILDING GODLY FAMILIES
    • We follow and commit ourselves to God's design for marriage and family in every way [ COLOSSIANS 3:13-15 | GALATIANS 5:13 | HEBREWS 10:24-25 ]
    • We aim to serve one another and to value kingdom community(local church) as a high priority [ JOHN 3:13-15 | GALATIANS 5:13 | HEBREWS 10:24-25]
    • We choose to commit ourselves to build meaningful relationships with other believers on all levels and to make every effort to keep these relationships alive[ EPHESIANS 4:3 ]
    • We believe the gifts of the Holy Spirit are fully functional [ 1 CORINTHIANS 12 ]



    "I NEED OTHER PEOPLE IN MY LIFE, FAMILY IS A PRIORITY"



  4. MISSION MINDED - CHOOSING A LIFE OF SACRIFICE FOR THE KINGDOM OF GOD
    • We embrace the truth that we are not here for ourselves [ MATTHEW 16:24 ]
    • We commit ourselves to adopt a 'going' mentality[ MATTHEW 28:19]
    • We allow our God given gifts and talents to be developed and commit to walk in our God given calling [ ROMANS 12:1 ]



    "I CANNOT KEEP THIS AMAZING TREASURE FOR MY LIFE, HAVE TO GIVE IT AWAY"



  5. CHRIST CENTERED - LIVING OUR LIVES TO WORSHIP HIM
    • We desire to live a life that brings honour to Jesus [ MARK 10:30 | COLOSSIANS 1:9-10 ]
    • We purpose to live with Christ like character including integrity, accountability, transparency, servanthood, perseverance and excellence [ JOHN 13:35, 15:13 | 1 JOHN 2:6]
    • We allow our God given gifts and talents to be developed and commit to walk in our God given calling [ ROMANS 12:1 ]



    "MY HIGHEST PURPOSE IS HIS HONOUR, GLORY, AND PRAISE AND TO FINISH THE RACE HE SET FOR ME"
